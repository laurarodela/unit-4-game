Crystal Collector Game

-A random number will be given to you at the start of the game

-Below are four crystals that each add a specific amount when selected, the points go towards your score

-You win the game when your total score matches with the given number

-You lose the game if your score surpasses the given number

-The value of the crystal is hidden and will reset at the beginning of every game
